# raw/

Immutable source material. The captain adds. AI reads. Nothing in here gets edited in place — if you need to revise, copy to `wiki/` and edit there.

## Subfolders

- `articles/` — PDFs, web clips, research docs, agreements
- `meeting-logs/` — Meeting transcripts, voice notes (`YYYY-MM-DD-topic.md`)
- `uploads/` — Files you drop here for AI context (anything multi-modal)
- `screenshots/` — Direct destination for your OS screenshot tool
- `data/` — CSVs, JSON exports, datasets

## Naming convention

Date-prefix everything that has a date: `2026-05-01-meeting-with-[name].md`. AI relies on filename dates when timeline-stitching.

## Why immutable?

Source material gets cited from wiki pages and from AI synthesis. If you edit raw material in place, your citations decay. Treat raw/ like a museum archive — read, copy out, never re-write.
